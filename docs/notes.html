My Written Notes/Thoughts

About This Page

This is my notes/thoughts page, where I post my written notes for subjects in mathematics and computer science and also some of my thoughts.

My ultimate goal is that my work can somewhat reduce the abstractness behind ideas in mathematics and computer science, making it more intuitive and easier to understand.

Analysis: Having a Successful Math Conversation

This is my set of notes intended for elementary analysis topics, corresponding to U of T courses MAT157Y, MAT257Y and MAY357S.

The unique part is I try to use natural language analogies for mathematics language in every possible way. I hope base on our daily experience in communication, we can then form good intuition behind these ideas of elementary analysis.
